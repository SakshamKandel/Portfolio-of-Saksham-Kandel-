About Me

I am a Lead Sports Designer dedicated to visualizing victory for Nepal's elite sports franchises. My work drives the brand identity of Nepal Premier League teams like the Biratnagar Kings and Karnali Yaks, while also shaping the visual presence of 3X3 Nepal for the Nepal Basketball Association.

Blending my background in Artificial Intelligence with creative design, I deliver forward-thinking branding, merchandise, and social media graphics that capture the raw energy of sports and elevate team presence.
